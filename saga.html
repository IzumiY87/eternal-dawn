📖 Sagas Overview

Saga 1: The Rise of Eyepatch Pirates

Izumi begins his journey to become the richest and most powerful pirate. He forms the Eyepatch Pirates, steals their ship, and recruits key members like Keal, Lena, and Sota. The saga focuses on escaping authorities, discovering cursed powers, and forming bonds.

Crew members introduced: Izumi, Keal, Lena, Sota, Isla

Notable events: Shipyard heist, first bounty reveals, curse awakening.

Saga 2: Mastery and Growth

After a 10-month time skip, the crew emerges stronger. Izumi gains full control of his cursed left eye after training with Sota. Keal becomes Vice Captain. Each member sharpens their abilities. They explore uncharted islands and face elite bounty hunters.

New powers revealed. Cursed relics hinted.

Time skip: 10 months

Saga 3: Storms and Shadows

The crew sails into a cursed archipelago and battles a rival pirate crew while uncovering pieces of the “Eternal Dawn.” The tension between the World Navy and the pirates rises. Sota unlocks a new cursed technique.

Hints at ancient war. New enemy introduced.
