Important Locations

Shroud Expanse

A vast and deadly sea region cloaked in perpetual fog, home to cursed storms and ancient shipwrecks. Eyepatch Pirates cross it during Saga 1 while escaping Soraya’s cursed fleet.

Isle of the Forgotten Sun

Once a thriving island, now a cursed wasteland buried in eternal twilight. Hosts the Vault of the Forgotten Sun and hidden cursed relics. Key location in Saga 2's climax where Dax betrays the crew.

Isle of Echoes

A mystical island that reflects memories and visions. Used by Sota to unlock Izumi’s past and help him confront his curse. Reached during the training period before Saga 2.

Flamewell Rift

A submerged volcanic trench said to be a birthplace of cursed flame. Accessible only by dive systems invented by Juno. Key to the divine awakening of the Twin Flamebearers in Saga 3.

Eclipse Haven

Hidden sanctuary only accessible during an eclipse. The location where Izumi and Izana reunite and face their divine trial. The sacred heart of the Sol’mar legacy.

Stormwater Bay

A port town known for shipbuilders, mercenaries, and secrets. This is where the Eyepatch Pirates steal their first ship in Chapter 3 and narrowly escape capture.

Pinecrest Island

A peaceful island with rich forests, often used as a temporary refuge by pirates. The crew rests and regroups here after their first major battle in Saga 1.
